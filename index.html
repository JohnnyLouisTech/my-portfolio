Welcome to the "About Me" section!

My Name is Johnny, and I fell in love with programming after taking Python, Java, Web Services, and MySQL while doing my IT undergraduate studies at SNHU. I was also introduced to Cloud Computing on AWS. Per the program curriculum, i used PyCharm and IntelliJ IDEA for IDEs. I then went on to learn HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, GIT/GitHub, Docker, Node.JS, jQuery, Django, and Jupyter for the back end, on my own. From there the learning never stops.

I describe myself as a partially self-taught web developer and software engineer. To date, I have created over 100 websites and some are short projects, sign up, cards, hamburger menu, etc.

When i'm not in front of my computer coding or learning about the newest technology, you can find me at the beach in a country where the temperature is over 100 deg.
